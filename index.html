Egyptian food recipes
Koshary
Koshary is considered the national dish of Egypt and a very popular street food. Egyptian koshari recipe is one of the easy Egyptian recipes.
Hawawshi
Hawawshi is basically crispy pita bread stuffed with kofta like mixture. It is a super easy dinner idea that is also perfect for parties and game night.
Falafel
Ta'ameya (Egyptian falafel) is an all-time favorite street food in Egypt. we make it with dried fava beans. These are best served with pita bread, tomato, onions, and tahini sauce.
Mahshi
Mahshi is one of the main platters in Egyptian banquets. It is by far the most common dish in Egypt. it is basically veggies stuffed with rice mixture
